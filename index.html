Hover over a county to show U.S. Census estimates of how many residents moved from the selected county to other counties over the past year.

A project by Ben Matheson.This uses U.S. Census American Community Survey data ("County-to-County Migration Flows: 2011-2015 ACS") Visualization utilizes D3. This project also draws heavy inspriation from the recent New York Times Upshot story How Connected Is Your Community to Everywhere Else in America?
